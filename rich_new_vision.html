RICH FOUR PRINCIPLES

My goal is to maintain a positive attitude.

EMPOWERING & INSPIRING THE ADULT GENERATION WITH RICH'S FOUR PRINCIPLES

RICH is a program that empowers young minds at every stage of their academic journey. Specifically for young adults transitioning to college or any adulthood stage, RICH focuses on promoting academic excellence and putting emphasis on the four principles each individual personalize their success. Through resources and mentorship, we and CUNY provide an environment that encourages individuality and celebrating unique perspectives. Our goal is to break down barriers, create opportunities for personal growth, and inspire the next generation of leaders and changemakers in their communities. Join RICH and CUNY to embark on a journey of personal-growth, discovery, and limitless possibilities.
